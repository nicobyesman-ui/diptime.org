What is DipTime™?

DipTime is a timing-domain encoding & streaming method. Instead of relying only on symbol values, we encode meaningful information in the spacing of events, enabling fewer on-air symbols for the same message. In our demo runs, we observed substantial “on-air unit” reductions and strong effective compression characteristics.

  • ✔️ Fewer transmitted symbols for the same payload
  • ✔️ Strong storage & energy savings indicators
  • ✔️ Works alongside classic ASCII/byte pipelines
  • ✔️ Targets data-heavy sectors (cloud, media, AI, satcoms)
